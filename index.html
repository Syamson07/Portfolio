Variable Refrigerant Flow Motor

Contributed to the electromagnetic design and optimization of a high-efficiency BLDC motor for Variable Refrigerant Flow HVAC applications. The objective was to achieve reliable operation, high efficiency, and smooth torque characteristics across the operating range.

My Role

  • Electromagnetic motor design
  • Performance analysis using FEA
  • Design optimization and validation

Design Approach

  1. Reviewed application requirements including speed, torque, efficiency, and operating conditions.
  2. Performed initial motor sizing calculations.
  3. Evaluated suitable slot-pole combinations and winding configurations.
  4. Developed electromagnetic models using Ansys Maxwell.
  5. Analyzed magnetic flux distribution, back-EMF, and torque characteristics.
  6. Optimized the design to reduce cogging torque and torque ripple.
  7. Coordinated thermal and performance validation activities.
  8. Supported prototype evaluation and design refinement.

Drone / Robotics BLDC Motor Development

Participated in the design and analysis of a lightweight BLDC motor for drone and robotics use, targeting high power density, fast dynamic response, and strong efficiency at low mass.

My Role

  • Electromagnetic design
  • Simulation and optimization
  • Performance analysis

Design Approach

  1. Defined performance targets including power, speed, and torque requirements.
  2. Performed preliminary electromagnetic sizing.
  3. Selected suitable slot-pole combinations for high power density.
  4. Built finite element models to evaluate magnetic performance.
  5. Analyzed back-EMF, torque production, and magnetic saturation.
  6. Optimized motor geometry for efficiency and weight reduction.
  7. Evaluated performance across different operating conditions.
  8. Supported prototype-oriented design decisions.
